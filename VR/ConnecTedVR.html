ConnecTed

A stylized 3D VR experience that chronicles Uncle Ted's life, starting with his experiences and work life during his pivotal years as he navigates a transforming country. The visitor will witness his most significant achievements and the struggles he faces while overcoming his toughest obstacles and life's unexpected challenges.

Release Date: 2025
